Other Events

Discover how you can have a truly unique experience by exploring
the wide range of events that are held at the Theatre of Dreams.



At Old Trafford, the home of Manchester United, the excitement doesn't end with
football matches. We take pride in offering a diverse range of unique events that set
us apart from the rest.

Whether you're a devoted football fan, visiting with your school, or simply looking
for an extraordinary day out, Old Trafford promises to deliver an unforgettable
experience that celebrates the beautiful game and the history of Manchester
United.
